Luminexus is an interactive digital platform designed to make learning about space weather fun and engaging. It helps students understand how the Sun affects Earth through activities, visuals, and stories that make complex science topics exciting and engaging discoveries. With Luminexus, users can explore how solar flares, coronal mass ejections, and other space events impact our planet and daily life.

At Luminexus, we believe that learning should spark curiosity and creativity. Our goal is to make science more accessible by blending education with interactivity and storytelling.
